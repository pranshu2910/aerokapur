About AeroKapur: Tradition Meets Innovation

At AeroKapur, we believe in the power of nature to purify and protect. Our journey begins with a single, remarkable ingredient: Camphor.

Derived from the resilient extracts of the pine tree, camphor has a rich, thousand-year history that is now fueling a vibrant, modern future. While deeply rooted in Indian culture for its pious and spiritual significance, camphor is emerging globally as a powerhouse for health, hygiene, and wellness.

Our Philosophy: Purity Without Compromise

In an era dominated by synthetic chemicals and artificial fragrances, AeroKapur stands for a return to organic integrity. We have reimagined the traditional Indian "Kapoor" into the AeroKapur Cone—a sophisticated, eco-friendly solution for the modern home and workspace.

The AeroKapur Difference

  • Pharmaceutical Grade Efficacy: Inspired by camphor’s role in orthopedic and respiratory pharmaceuticals—same medicinal-grade purity.
  • The New Standard in Hygiene: Replacing outdated chemicals like naphthalene and PDCB with non-toxic, biodegradable alternatives.
  • Advanced Formulation: Age-old wisdom + modern R&D: our "control-release" cones purify air, repel pests, and elevate mood without harmful aerosols.

Our Vision for the Future

Historically, Indian camphor was reserved for the altar. At AeroKapur, we are expanding that horizon—pioneering innovative hygiene products that bring the therapeutic and protective benefits of camphor into every car, wardrobe, and living space.

Join us as we redefine freshness—turning a sacred tradition into a global essential for a healthier, chemical-free lifestyle.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes AeroKapur different from regular room fresheners?

Most commercial air fresheners use synthetic chemicals and aerosols that only mask odors. AeroKapur is made from 100% organic, pine-derived camphor. It purifies the air, provides therapeutic benefits, and is completely non-toxic.

How long does one AeroKapur cone last?

Thanks to our controlled-release formulation, one cone typically lasts 45 to 60 days, depending on room size and airflow.

Is it safe to use around children and pets?

Yes! Unlike naphthalene balls or PDCB-based products, camphor is a natural organic compound. AeroKapur is safe for homes with kids and pets when hung where they cannot directly reach or swallow it.

Where is the best place to hang the AeroKapur cone?

Cars: Hang from the rearview mirror. Wardrobes: Keeps clothes fresh and prevents silverfish/moths. Bathrooms: Neutralizes odors and dampness. Office Desks: Helps focus and clears the mind.

Does it really repel mosquitoes?

Yes. Camphor has been used for centuries as a natural repellent. The constant release from the AeroKapur cone creates an environment that mosquitoes and flies naturally avoid.

Will the camphor cone disappear over time?

Yes. Camphor is a sublimating substance—it turns from solid to vapor. When the cone is empty, replace it with a fresh one to maintain aroma and protection.

What is the minimum order quantity?

Minimum order is 3 units. We offer a special price of just ₹100 per unit for great value!

Pro-Tip: If the scent feels too strong in a small car, keep the outer plastic packaging partially on to slow down the release!
