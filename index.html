About S.S Creation & AeroKapur

Crafted with Care

Welcome to AeroKapur, proudly presented by S.S Creation. Based in the heart of Bhilai, we are dedicated to bringing the pure, traditional benefits of camphor into modern, convenient formats.

Our organic camphor cones are crafted with care to not only eliminate stubborn odors but to also spread a soothing, positive energy in your surroundings. We believe in natural living, which is why our products are free from harsh chemicals, offering you a safe, refreshing, and long-lasting experience.
